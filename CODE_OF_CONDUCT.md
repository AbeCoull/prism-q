# Code of Conduct

PRISM-Q is a performance oriented project. Strong disagreement is welcome
when it is grounded in measurements, correctness, and clear reasoning.

## Standards

- Be direct, useful, and respectful.
- Critique code, benchmarks, designs, and claims, not people.
- Bring evidence when making technical claims.
- Keep issues, reviews, and discussions on topic.
- Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, sexualize discussion, publish private
  information, or disrupt project spaces.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report concerns privately through repository contact or security channels.
Include relevant links, dates, and screenshots when useful.

Maintainers may remove comments, restrict participation, or take other action
proportional to the issue. This policy can be updated as the project grows.
